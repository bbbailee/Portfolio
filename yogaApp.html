Calm Yoga

An app for young children to benefit from the positive effects of practising yoga

Figma File
Figma Prototype

Brief

The brief of this project was to create a mobile app for children to practise and learn yoga.

Solution

An app that is easy for children to use, colourful and addresses the struggles many parents have with calming their children down.

Process

1

Research

This includes reasearch on the topic, target audience, competitors, current app designs and design trends. Research is essential in starting any project and creating good base knowledge to design from.

2

Ideating

Ideating is the start of the design process. It includes brainstorming the topic and ideas, concept sketching. In this project I used a method called Deck of Brilliance to think outside of the box when coming up with design ideas.

3

Designing concepts

To start concept designing, I do colour palette testing, typographical testing, mood boards for multiple concepts, and layout testing. In this project, I created three different concepts to choose from, and picked the stongest design for the brief.

4

Final Design

Creating the final design includes finalising and polishing elements and screen layouts in Figma, and prototyping the flow of the app.

Tools
