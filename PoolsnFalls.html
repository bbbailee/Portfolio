Pools & Falls

A website that showcases the best swimming locations in Australia.

Brief

A website that will list places around Australia that people can visit and enjoy a swim.

Solution

A website that helps users discover new locations they haven't heard of, and be prepared for visiting these locations with all the basic information they need.

Process

1

Research
Topic, target audience and competitor, app designs, design trends research are essential in starting any project

2

Ideating
Brainstorming, Deck of brilliance, concept sketching

3

Designing concepts
colour paletee testing, tyupographical testing, concept mood boards, layout testing

4

Final Design
Creating elements and screen layouts in Figma.
