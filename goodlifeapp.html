Goodlife App

An app for people to find exercises and engage in group fitness activites

Figma File
Figma Prototype

Brief

A fitness app where Goodlife members can find workouts, and social groups where they can workout with other members.

Solution

An app for members to find, save and record workouts, and meet like minded groups to enjoy exercise with.

Process

1

Research

This includes researching the topic and the target audience, scoping out competitor apps and existing app designs, and reserching current design trends.

2

Ideating

Brainstorming, rouch layout sketches and using research to inspire ideas and creative ways of displaying information.

3

Designing concept

This includes colour palette testing, typographical testing and designing the strongest ideas from the rough sketches.

4

Final Design

This involves creating elements and screen layouts in Figma and prototyping the user flow. It also involves creating device mock ups to visualise the design in the context of using it on a phone.

Persona

Jake
28
Electrician
Jake just moved to Albany, WA for work. He usually spends his free time with friends and surfing, but doesn't know anyone. He wants a way to find people with similar hobbies so he can make friends and stay fit.

Tools

I used Figma to create the final design of the app, and online resources such as Behance and Dribbble for inspiration in my layouts.
